About

With over 20 years of experience, we are proud to be a trusted name in gardening and landscape care. Based in the Sussex area, our business has been built from small beginnings with real hands-on experience. We believe that working closely with our clients helps achieve the best and most desired results.

Partnered with leading heritage and nature charities, we are honoured to care for some of the nation’s most treasured landscapes. From historic estates to modern green spaces, we bring the same attention and commitment to every project.

We're happy to consider all types of work. We are accomplished and bring a wealth of knowledge in both soft and hard landscaping.

We cater to both domestic and commercial clients and always consider jobs of all sizes. With £10 million public liability insurance, nothing is left to chance — enabling us to confidently work on larger commercial sites as well as private gardens.

  • Regular garden maintenance
  • Lawn care and turfing
  • Tree planting and tree surgery

  • Planting schemes and seasonal displays
  • Weeding, pruning, and soil care
  • Garden clearance and waste removal
  • Wildlife garden and habitat creation

  • Fencing, decking, and outdoor structures
